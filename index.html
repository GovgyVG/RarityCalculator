FAQ & Guide

What is this calculator for?
This tool helps you accurately calculate the final rarity, ATK, and HP of your slimes based on their base rarity, current level, and active mutations.
How do mutations work?
Each mutation multiplies your slime's rarity and stats. By combining specific mutations (like Shiny + Big), you can trigger "Presets" which offer massively increased multipliers compared to separate mutations.
Why can't I select some mutations together?
Certain mutations are mutually exclusive and cannot exist on the same slime at once. For example, a slime cannot be both Big and Huge simultaneously.
How are the stats calculated?
Stats scale continuously with the slime's level modifier and mutation multipliers. The base stats are scaled by your slime's level up to Lv. 9999. The stat multiplier depends on your specific combination of mutations.
How do I save my Favorite slimes?
Once you configure your slime, level, and mutations, click the "☆ Save" button. It will be stored in your browser locally. You can easily click any saved slime to instantly load its configuration, or simply drag and drop them to reorder your Favorites list.
